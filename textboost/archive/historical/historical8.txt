The Odyssey by Homer (c. 8th century BCE) - Another epic poem attributed to Homer, the Odyssey follows the Greek hero Odysseus on his ten-year journey back to Ithaca after the fall of Troy. The poem's themes include perseverance, temptation, and the complexities of the human condition. It remains a classic in world literature, exploring the challenges of returning home after war and the enduring power of storytelling.
