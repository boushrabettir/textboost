The Mayflower Compact (1620) - The Mayflower Compact was a pioneering agreement signed by the Pilgrims aboard the Mayflower ship on November 11, 1620, before disembarking at Plymouth, Massachusetts. It established a temporary government and social contract to maintain order and govern the new settlement democratically. The Mayflower Compact laid the groundwork for the principles of self-governance and the rule of law in American colonial history.
