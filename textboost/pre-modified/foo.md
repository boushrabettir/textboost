# FIFA World Cup

The FIFA World Cup is held every four years and is the pinnacle of international soccer. Teams from all over the world compete to win the prestigious trophy.

- The tournament lasts about one month.
- 32 teams participate in the finals.
- Matches are played in different cities of the host country.
- Fans from around the world attend to support their national teams.

Soccer is played with two teams of eleven players each. The objective is to score more goals than the opposing team.

- A goal is scored when the ball crosses the opponent's goal line.
- The tournament also awards the Golden Boot to the top scorer.
- Players often train for years to compete at this level.

The FIFA World Cup showcases talent, teamwork, and national pride.
